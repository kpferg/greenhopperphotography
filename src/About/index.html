I picked up my first camera in elementary school to try to capture my field trip "adventures" and 4th of July fireworks. Growing up in Maine gave me plenty of beautiful landscapes to learn photography as a hobby. In High School I was more comfortable behind the camera than writing articles for the school newspaper so I took on the challenge of photo editor.

Today I enjoy bringing my love for photography and the technical aspects of creating beautiful and powerful images to the digital realm. I'm available for weddings, events, family and couple photo shoots as well as adventure photography in places that you wouldn't always think to bring a camera. I'd love to hear what project you have in mind and help make it become a reality for you.

- Kevin Ferguson
